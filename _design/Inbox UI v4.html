What changed from v3

One avatar, one tone

  • Every avatar — pinned + list — is a flat cream-tan circle (#E8DDC8) with a Fraunces italic monogram in tan. No gradient, no shadow, no inversion when on a tinted card.
  • Tiny sage live dot sits centered under a pinned tile (not on the circle itself), iMessage parity.

One warmth, one place

  • Canvas is pale warm paper (#F8F4EC). Active chats are white cards. Pending reach-outs are soft peach-cream cards — the only surface in the inbox that's warm.
  • Pending preview switches to Fraunces italic, so the reach-out reads as a quote from them.

One chip family

  • All status chips are ink-on-cream: Reply, Hired, and the countdown. Countdown flips from outline → solid ink when under 24h left. No terracotta, no rouge — keeps the inbox quiet.

Type hierarchy borrowed from editorial

  • Small-caps ALL-CAPS context line with bold project name + bullet separator + role ("LATE NIGHT RADIO · REACHED OUT TO YOU"). Reads as a byline.
  • Name in Fraunces serif at 19pt, weight 600.
  • Preview body in Plus Jakarta 13.5pt; pending preview switches to Fraunces italic.
